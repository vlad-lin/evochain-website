# EvoChain: Business Capabilities & Results

EvoChain is an intuitive, industry-agnostic supply chain operating system designed for multi-echelon small and medium business owners. It leverages AI and Machine Learning to design the supply chain network, optimize inventory, improve forecast quality, and generate actionable end-to-end replenishment plans.

## Core Business Results

* **Frictionless Onboarding:** Provides instant, isolated database workspaces for rapid deployment and secure multi-tenant planning.
* **Automated Data Ingestion:** Eliminates manual data entry by using AI to automate the mapping and zero-filling of unstructured sales and inventory data.
* **Improved Forecast Quality:** Generates highly accurate demand baselines using time-series Machine Learning models, complete with stratified backtesting to measure Forecast Value Added (FVA).
* **Optimized Inventory Levels:** Calculates optimal inventory buffers by applying stochastic mathematical modeling for Finished Goods and deterministic buffering for Raw Materials.
* **Actionable End-to-End Replenishment (MRP):** Produces time-phased purchasing and production plans that automatically offset supplier lead times, respect Minimum Order Quantities (MOQs), consume projected inventory, and dynamically explode Bills of Material (BOM).
* **Strategic Network Design:** Empowers long-term scenario planning at the facility level, allowing businesses to simulate and optimize inbound, outbound, and warehousing costs.
* **Sales Pipeline Integration (CRM):** Utilizes Opportunity Delta Reversal logic to seamlessly integrate won deals and S&OP consensus forecasts directly into active ML demand baselines.
* **Capacity & Execution Management:** Delivers rough-cut capacity planning (RCCP) for shop floor loads and network dispatching logic to mathematically identify and rebalance inventory imbalances across locations.
* **Collaborative Planning (CPFR):** Enables tight alignment with key accounts and strategic suppliers by sharing consensus forecasts and tracking committed delivery requirements.
